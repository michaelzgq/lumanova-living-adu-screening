Start with the city path, not a generic legalization assumption

合法化类客户最容易误以为 “只要在 LA 就都一样”，或者 “LA City 有 UDU，所以所有非法单元都能走”。这两种说法都不稳。

LA City has specific programs, but they are not universal

LA City has official paths for certain unpermitted dwelling situations, but not every converted garage or detached room automatically belongs there.

LA City 的确有 UDU 这类官方入口，但它只适用于符合条件的 unpermitted units，不是所有旧 garage / detached room 都自动适用。

County and SGV cities may route very differently

Unincorporated County, Arcadia, San Gabriel, Monterey Park, and Pasadena do not share one legalization script. The same-sounding issue can require a different first move.

LA County、Arcadia、San Gabriel、Monterey Park、Pasadena 没有一套统一合法化剧本。听起来相似的问题，也可能要走完全不同的第一步。

A Los Angeles mailing address does not automatically mean LA City, and LA City does not mean every existing issue can be explained as a UDU case.

Common situations this screen is for

只要是旧车库、无 permit 的 living unit、旧加建、sale-time issue、correction notice 这类高摩擦问题，都比普通 remodel 咨询更适合从这里进。

Correction notice or sale-time issue

If an agent, buyer, lender, or city notice surfaced a permit problem, this is the right entry point.

What this screen does

这个 screen 的价值，不是替客户下结论，而是先把问题类型、城市路径和风险等级分出来，让人工知道该继续联系、先查 records，还是升级处理。

  • Collects the property address, project goal, and known blocker signals.
  • Separates standard-looking ADU cases from cases that likely need deeper records review or a different compliance path.
  • Gives the team enough context to decide whether to call, review, or escalate.

This is a screening tool, not a final permit opinion, legal opinion, or engineering judgment.

What this screen does not do

这里必须把边界说清：它不是法律意见、不是 permit guarantee，也不是工程判断。复杂项目最终还是要回到城市官网、designer、architect 或内部查询团队。

It does not replace city confirmation

Use the screen to sort the project early, not to assume a final permit outcome without records review or official confirmation.

It does not turn a complex case into a simple one

Older work, permit gaps, hillside conditions, and correction history still need real review even if the first intake looks promising.

What to do before you hire anyone

在合法化类场景里，最怕的是先去花 architect / attorney / contractor 的钱，却还没搞清自己到底属于哪个城市系统、哪种问题类型。

  • Do not assume an old converted unit will move like a clean new ADU project.
  • Do not assume a seller-side or notice-driven issue automatically fits one LA City program without checking jurisdiction and property type.
  • Do not spend on a full design package before understanding whether the existing conditions create extra friction.
  • Do not call it “simple legalization” until the property details and records are screened.

FAQ

Should I call the city first?

Not usually as a first move. It is often better to screen the property details and the likely issue type before treating the case like a simple city question.

Does every LA City unpermitted unit go through the same path?

No. LA City has specific official entry points, but not every converted garage, detached room, or old unit automatically fits the same program. Address, property type, and records still come first.

Is this only for severe problem cases?

No. It also works for people who just discovered that an older garage, detached room, or permit history may complicate a sale, refinance, or future project.

What happens after I submit?

The intake is routed for manual review. If the case looks workable, someone can follow up with a better sense of whether it belongs in a cleaner path or a deeper review path.
