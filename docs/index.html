What this is

这是什么

This is not a final permit opinion and not a construction quote form. It is an early property screen that helps separate cleaner-looking projects from blocker-heavy cases, older unpermitted work, or situations that need deeper records review.

这不是最终 permit 结论，也不是装修报价页。它更像一个前置分诊工具，先把地址、城市入口、 项目类型和 blocker 信号看清楚，再决定该继续走标准路径、先做 blocker diagnosis， 还是按救援 / 合法化思路处理。

Built for real homeowner questions

围绕真实业主问题设计

Most people do not start with a clean permit strategy. They start with one of these questions: “Can I convert this garage?”, “Is this lot even workable?”, or “What do I do about an old unit built without permits?”

大多数业主一开始不会带着清晰的 permit 路线进来，而是先问： “这个车库能不能改？”“这块地到底能不能做？”“旧的无 permit 单元现在该怎么办？”

Why address and city come first

为什么地址和城市要放在最前面

The most valuable early question is not just “can I do it?” It is whether the property belongs to LA City, LA County, or a separate SGV city system.

很多客户一开口就问 “能不能做” 或 “大概多少钱”，但真正更早、更值钱的问题通常是： 这个地址到底属于 LA City、LA County，还是 Arcadia、Pasadena、San Gabriel、Monterey Park 这样的独立城市。

LA City is not the same as LA County

LA City 和 LA County 不是一回事

A Los Angeles mailing address can still belong to County or another city workflow.

邮寄地址写 Los Angeles，不等于一定属于 LA City。很多客户实际归 County 或其他城市系统。

SGV cities do not use one shared playbook

圣盖博谷各城市不是一套剧本

Arcadia, San Gabriel, Monterey Park, and Pasadena each have their own entry points and review style.

Arcadia、San Gabriel、Monterey Park、Pasadena 各自有不同的入口和审核逻辑。

Path names depend on the real situation

路径名称取决于真实情况

Not every old unit belongs in the same legalization or ADU path.

不是每个现有问题都叫合法化，也不是每个旧车库都适合直接讲 ADU 路径。
