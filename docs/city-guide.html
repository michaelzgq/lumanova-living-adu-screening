City / official entry guide for LA City, LA County, and key SGV cities

LA City、LA County 和重点 SGV 城市的官方入口指南

This page is built to answer one early question correctly: where should this property be checked first? It does not replace technical review. It helps homeowners and agents avoid the most common mistake, which is assuming every Los Angeles mailing address belongs to the same city path.

这个页面只做一件事：帮你先找对官方入口。它不替代 designer、architect 或 city confirmation， 但能先避免最常见的错误：把邮寄地址写 Los Angeles 的房子都当成 LA City，或者把 Arcadia、Pasadena、 San Gabriel、Monterey Park 当成一套统一规则。

Three rules homeowners and agents should remember

业主和 agent 都该记住的三条规则

Do not answer the city question from memory

不要凭记忆回答城市问题

Use the official page first, then speak.

不要凭经验替代城市官网，先查入口，再解释路径。

Do not mix one city with another

不要把一个城市的逻辑套到另一个城市

Arcadia logic should not be used to explain Pasadena or LA City.

不能把一个城市的规则直接套到另一个城市，尤其不能把 Pasadena 讲成 LA City。

Do not turn an official page into a guarantee

不要把官网入口当成结果保证

Official entry points tell you where to start, not how the final technical answer will end.

官网入口告诉你“先去哪查”，不代表最终结果已经确定。

What to do if the city is still unclear

如果城市还不确定，该怎么做

  • Use the property screen first and submit the exact address.
  • Flag whether the issue is garage conversion, detached ADU, or an existing unpermitted unit.
  • Do not make permit or legalization promises until jurisdiction is confirmed.
  • 先提交物业预筛，把准确地址交进来。
  • 标清楚这更像 garage conversion、detached ADU，还是现有非法单元问题。
  • 在 jurisdiction 没确认前，不要做 permit 或合法化承诺。
