The first question is usually not cost. It is jurisdiction.

车库改造类客户最容易一开始就问成本，但销售更该先判断：这个地址到底是 LA City、LA County，还是 Arcadia、San Gabriel、 Monterey Park、Pasadena 这样的独立城市。入口错了，后面的 permit 话术就会全错。

LA City garage questions

LA City often means LADBS plus Planning context, and some garage or existing-unit questions split into very different paths depending on the actual records and property type.

LA City 常见入口是 `LADBS + Planning`，但具体是 clean garage path、UDU 相关问题，还是一般 compliance / permit 问题，要看真实记录。

County and SGV city questions

Unincorporated LA County, Arcadia, San Gabriel, Monterey Park, and Pasadena are not one interchangeable system. A garage path that sounds simple in one city can become a different review in another.

County 和 SGV 独立城市不能混讲。某个城市看起来是标准车库改造，在另一个城市可能就变成更重的 review。

A Los Angeles mailing address does not automatically mean LA City. Pasadena also cannot be explained as if it were LA City.

What usually moves project cost up or down

这里不直接挂固定报价，因为真实成本不是一句 “garage conversion 多少钱” 就能回答。更关键的是先看 scope、城市审核、旧记录和隐藏 blocker。

  • How clean or messy the existing garage and permit history look
  • Whether utilities, service upgrades, or extra site work are needed
  • Whether the project stays close to a clean garage conversion or expands into a bigger scope
  • Which city or jurisdiction reviews the project and how much friction the path creates
  • Whether old additions, corrections, hillside issues, or other blocker signals appear early

We are not publishing fixed price ranges here yet. Actual cost depends on scope, records, city review, utilities, and existing site conditions.

What usually makes a project more complex

真正把项目拖复杂的，往往不是装修本身，而是 hillside、basement、旧加建、无 permit 施工和 correction / violation 这类历史问题。

  • Hillside or special slope conditions
  • Basement or below-grade living space
  • Old additions without permits
  • Existing unpermitted work or unclear permit history
  • Correction comments, prior notices, or existing records mismatch

In San Gabriel and other older SGV housing pockets, legacy work and records friction often matter more than the first construction question.

City patterns homeowners should not mix together

这几座城市听起来都在 SGV，但销售和客户不能把它们讲成一套统一规则。先找对城市官网，再谈下一步。

Arcadia

Arcadia can look cleaner for standard ADU-style exploration, but project fit still depends on the lot and category. A standard-sounding garage idea is not automatically approved just because the city has clearer public entry points.

Arcadia 的 ADU 入口相对清楚，甚至有预批准 ADU 这类卖点，但不是所有 garage 情况都能直接套标准路径。

Monterey Park and San Gabriel

Older homes and historical work are common, so record history can matter as much as the physical garage itself.

Monterey Park 和 San Gabriel 常见老屋和历史遗留问题，很多时候记录和现状比施工本身更重要。

Pasadena

Pasadena has its own path and cannot be treated like LA City. Historic or city-specific review can change what looks simple on the surface.

Pasadena 不能按 LA City 去解释，尤其碰到历史保护或 city-specific review 时，销售不能先替客户下结论。

What homeowners usually want to know first

客户真正关心的不是只有价格，而是这个 garage 项目到底像不像标准路径，会不会碰到 utilities、records 或 city review friction。

Is this likely a clean garage path?

Some properties stay close to a cleaner garage-conversion scenario. Others immediately show record, site, or scope friction.

What slows a project down?

Hillside conditions, utility work, old additions, and mismatched records tend to change both timing and next-step strategy.

Should I get contractor quotes now?

Not as a first move. A better first move is screening the property so you know whether the project looks standard, blocked, or heavier than expected.

Why screening first matters

The right first step is not getting three random construction quotes. The right first step is finding out whether the property looks closer to a standard path, a blocker-heavy review, or a legalization-type case.

正确的第一步通常不是去找三个 contractor 乱比价，而是先判断这个项目更像标准 garage path、blocker-heavy review， 还是已经偏合法化 / 救援场景。

FAQ

Does this page give me a price quote?

No. This page is meant to help you understand what usually affects scope and complexity. Real cost depends on the property, records, city review, utilities, and hidden blockers.

Is every garage eligible?

No. Even before detailed design, some properties show signs that they belong in a blocker-diagnosis path instead of a cleaner standard path.

Are LA City, Pasadena, Arcadia, and SGV garage rules basically the same?

No. The state framework may sound similar, but the real entry point, review friction, and likely next step still depend on the actual jurisdiction and property conditions.

What happens after I submit the screen?

The intake helps route the project so the team has a better idea whether to treat it like a cleaner garage path or a heavier review case before moving deeper.
